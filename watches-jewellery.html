Useful answers

Before You Bring It In

Quick answers to help customers understand what to expect.

Do I need an appointment?

No. Walk-ins are welcome, but a WhatsApp photo first can help us guide you faster.

Can every item be fixed or polished?

Not always. It depends on the item condition, material and damage. We check first and explain honestly.

Do you replace watch batteries?

We handle watch battery enquiries in store. Availability and suitability depend on the watch type.

Can you adjust bracelets?

Simple bracelet adjustment support may be possible. Bring the item in so we can check safely.
