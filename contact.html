Useful answers

Before You Contact Us

These answers reduce repeated questions and help customers choose the right contact method.

Can I walk in without booking?

Yes, walk-ins are welcome during opening hours. For repairs or jewellery items, a WhatsApp photo first can help us guide you faster.

What is the fastest way to get a reply?

WhatsApp is usually the fastest during shop hours, especially if you need to send a photo or device model.

Can I email documents for printing?

Yes. Email your documents to mhconnect.uk2026@gmail.com and include whether you need colour or black and white, number of copies and collection time.
