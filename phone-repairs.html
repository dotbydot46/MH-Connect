Useful answers

Before You Visit

These quick answers help customers understand the repair journey before they come to the shop.

Do I need an appointment?

No. Walk-ins are welcome, but WhatsApp first is best if you want a quick quote or to check part availability.

How long does a repair take?

It depends on the model, issue and part stock. Some repairs may be same-day where possible.

Do repairs come with warranty?

Warranty depends on repair type and part used. We explain the warranty before any repair starts.

Can I send a photo?

Yes. A clear photo on WhatsApp helps us understand cracked screen, back glass, charging port or visible damage.

Do you repair iPhone and Samsung?

Yes, we handle enquiries for popular iPhone, Samsung and Android models, depending on parts and repair type.
