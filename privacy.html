Who we are

MH Connect is a local shop at 59 Lower Addiscombe Road, Croydon CR0 6PQ. You can contact us at mhconnect.uk2026@gmail.com or 07398 770915.

What information we collect

When you contact us, we may receive your name, phone number, email address, service request, device model, document details, photos you choose to send, and any message you provide.

How we use it

We use your details only to respond to your enquiry, prepare a quote, check stock, arrange a repair, help with printing, or provide customer support related to your request.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry information only for as long as reasonably needed to respond, provide the service, keep basic business records, or deal with follow-up questions.

Sharing

We do not sell your information. We may use normal communication services such as WhatsApp, email and phone providers to handle your messages.

This website is currently designed to work without unnecessary tracking. If analytics or advertising tools are added later, the website should explain this clearly and ask for consent where required.

Your choices

You can ask us about the information we hold from your enquiry, request correction, or ask us to delete unnecessary enquiry details by contacting us.
